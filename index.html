Let's talk about what we offer

Multistate Energy Coop is in the process of acquiring 100% equity stake of standstill (Lanco) Teesta-VI HEP (4X125) 500 MW from the Lenders of The Project and shall develop the project with stakeholders mainly comprising of domestic energy consumers.

See Details


Equity Stake

Multistate Energy Coop is offering equity stake to Energy Consumers of Sikkim and West Bengal in Teesta-VI HEP revival plan through a tailor-made scheme known as the Kilowatt Energy Share Scheme (KESS).

Membership Fee

Return to Members

On exemption of Income Tax and Dividend Distribution Tax members will get 13.70% Internal Rate of Return on account of dividend income and redemption of Equity Shares at the end of project life.

Let's talk about us




MULTISTATE ENERGY CONSUMERS’ CUM PRODUCERS’ CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETY LIMITED (Multistate Energy Coop) is the first power cooperative registered under the Multi-State Co-operative Societies Act, 2002, Ministry of Cooperation, Government of India. Vide Registration No. MSCS/CR/1251/2016 with its registered office at Upper Burtuk, Gangtok, Sikkim.The authorised share capital of Multistate Energy Coop is Rs.500 Crores with 5 million shares at a face value of Rs.1000 each. The borrowing power is ten-times of its paid-up share capital.
