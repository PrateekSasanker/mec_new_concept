Let's talk about us




MULTISTATE ENERGY CONSUMERS’ CUM PRODUCERS’ CO-OPERATIVE SOCIETY LIMITED (Multistate Energy Coop) is the first power cooperative registered under the Multi-State Co-operative Societies Act, 2002, Ministry of Cooperation, Government of India. Vide Registration No. MSCS/CR/1251/2016 with its registered office at Upper Burtuk, Gangtok, Sikkim.The authorised share capital of Multistate Energy Coop is Rs.500 Crores with 5 million shares at a face value of Rs.1000 each. The borrowing power is ten-times of its paid-up share capital.
